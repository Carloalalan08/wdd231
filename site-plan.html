Site Plan: Bigger Whale Printing Press

Site Name

Bigger Whale — This name is inspired by the business’s brand identity. It reflects a bold and memorable brand, making it easy for customers to recognize and associate with quality and creativity in printing.

Optional Domain: https://carloalalan08.github.io/wdd231/bigger_whale.html

Site Purpose

The website will serve as the official online platform for Bigger Whale, a printing press business offering high-quality design and print services. The site will:

  • Provide an overview of services such as business cards, flyers, brochures, and tarpaulins.
  • Showcase the company’s design portfolio to attract potential clients.
  • Allow users to request quotes or send inquiries through a contact form.
  • Share customer testimonials and business updates to boost credibility and engagement.

Scenarios

  • How can I request a custom quote for a brochure or tarpaulin design?
  • Can I view some of the previous design works completed by Bigger Whale?
  • Where is Bigger Whale located, and how can I contact or visit the shop?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #F31976 – Used for links, buttons, and highlight elements.
  • Accent Color: #19236D – Used for headings, borders, and emphasis.
  • Background: #FDA7E9 – Overall page background for a clean, neutral aesthetic.

Typography

  • Abril Fatface – Used for all headings to give a bold, modern look.
  • Lato – Used for body text for readability and simplicity.
